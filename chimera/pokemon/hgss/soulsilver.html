Why so extra?

This save file started with a lucky shiny, and pushed me to do a lot of things I would soon miss after the onset of physical disability. It's accompanied me through massive changes and became something I could devote myself to when I was in too much pain to do much else. That's something I'd had a hard time capturing since I was a child playing this game for the first time! All the memories I've made with it hold a special place in my heart.
